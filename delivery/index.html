Digital Delivery Policy
Last Updated Nov 26, 2023
  • Delivery of Architectural Plans:
    • Upon successful payment, your requested architectural plans will be delivered directly through our app. Here's how the process works:
  • Order Processing:
    • Plans will be processed within 3-4 business days of receiving the order and payment confirmation.
  • Delivery Method:
    • Plans will be delivered electronically through our app.
    • Customers will receive a notification within the app once the plans are ready for download.
  • Access Instructions:
    • To access your architectural plans, log in to the app using the account used during the purchase.
    • Navigate to the "Projects" or a similar section within the app to find and download your plans.
  • Turnaround Time:
    • We aim to provide a quick turnaround time for the creation and delivery of architectural plans. The standard processing time is 3-4 business days.
  • Factors Affecting Delivery:
    • Factors such as the complexity of the plan, additional customization, or unforeseen technical issues may impact the delivery time.
  • Order Status and Communication:
    • Customers can check the status of their orders within the app.
    • Any communication regarding order status or additional information will be sent through in-app notifications or email.
  • Compliance with RBI Mandated Rules:
    • Our digital delivery procedures adhere to RBI-mandated rules, and we ensure strict compliance during our verification processes.
